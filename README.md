## **Standards & Frameworks**

*Foundational specifications and open-source standards defining the green digital economy.*

* [**Sustainable Software Carbon Intensity (SCI)**](https://github.com/Green-Software-Foundation/sci) - A specification that describes how to calculate a **Software Carbon Intensity (SCI)** score for software applications, essential for auditing the footprint of blockchain nodes and dApps.
* [**Token Taxonomy Framework (TTF)**](https://github.com/InterWorkAlliance/TokenTaxonomyFramework) - Managed by the **InterWork Alliance (GBBC)**, this framework provides a common language and toolset for defining tokenized assets, including specific profiles for carbon credits and ESG assets.
* [**Blockchain Carbon Accounting**](https://github.com/hyperledger-labs/blockchain-carbon-accounting) - An open-source initiative hosted by the Linux Foundation to develop a shared ledger for carbon accounting and emissions tracking.
* [**Carbon Aware SDK**](https://github.com/Green-Software-Foundation/carbon-aware-sdk) - A tool from the Green Software Foundation that helps developers build software that runs when and where electricity is cleanest (e.g., executing heavy smart contracts during high renewable grid availability).

## **Energy Grid & P2P Trading**

*Software for decentralized energy grids, renewable energy certificates (RECs), and peer-to-peer trading.*

* [**Energy Web Origin**](https://github.com/energywebfoundation/origin) - An open-source SDK and set of toolkits for building decentralized applications for issuing and managing **Energy Attribute Certificates (EACs)**.
* [**Energy Web DOS**](https://github.com/energywebfoundation) - The latest decentralized operating system (DOS) from Energy Web, designed to facilitate worker node coordination for grid flexibility and validation. 1
* [**Switchboard dApp**](https://github.com/energywebfoundation/switchboard-dapp) - The identity and access management interface for the Energy Web ecosystem, allowing assets (batteries, EVs) to have self-sovereign identities (DID).
* [**Power Ledger (Powr)**](https://www.powerledger.io/) - (Proprietary/Enterprise) A leading platform for P2P energy trading, deployed in Thailand (T77 precinct) and India, allowing prosumers to trade excess solar power. 2

## **Digital Carbon Markets (ReFi)**

*Protocols and liquidity engines for the tokenization and trading of carbon credits.*

* [**Carbonmark API**](https://github.com/KlimaDAO/klimadao) - Developed by **KlimaDAO**, this open-source API allows developers to programmatically source, trade, and retire verified carbon credits directly from their applications.
* [**Toucan SDK**](https://github.com/ToucanProtocol/toucan-sdk) - A TypeScript SDK for interacting with the **Toucan Protocol**, enabling the bridging of off-chain carbon credits (like Verra) into on-chain tokens (TCO2).
* [**Toucan Contracts**](https://github.com/ToucanProtocol/contracts) - The core smart contracts for Toucan's Carbon Bridge and Carbon Pools, facilitating deep liquidity for environmental assets.
* [**KlimaDAO Contracts**](https://github.com/KlimaDAO/klimadao) - The smart contract repository for KlimaDAO, including the automated market maker (AMM) logic that drives the digital carbon market.

## **Digital MRV (Measurement, Reporting, Verification)**

*Tools that use IoT and AI to prove environmental impact on-chain.*

* [**Hedera Guardian**](https://github.com/hashgraph/guardian) - A massive open-source Policy Workflow Engine (PWE) that digitizes ESG methodologies. It creates auditable, tokenized quantitative outcomes (like carbon offsets) using the Hedera Consensus Service.
* [**Open Forest Protocol (OFP)**](https://www.openforestprotocol.org/) - A scalable, open protocol that allows forest projects to Measure, Report, and Verify forestation data using blockchain for transparency.
* [**GainForest**](https://github.com/GainForest) - A decentralized fund using Artificial Intelligence to measure and reward sustainable nature stewardship, compatible with Solana and Ethereum.
* [**Veritree**](https://veritree.com/) - (Cardano-based) A planting verification platform that uses blockchain to secure tree planting data, preventing double-counting and ensuring permanence.

## **Supply Chain & Circular Economy**

*Tracking materials, waste, and product lifecycles.*

* [**IOTA DPP Demonstrator**](https://github.com/iotaledger/dpp-demonstrator) - An open-source reference implementation for Digital Product Passports (DPP) using IOTA's Tangle to track product history and recyclability compliance.
* [**Hyperledger Fabric**](https://github.com/hyperledger/fabric) - The industry standard for enterprise permissioned blockchains, widely used for private supply chain tracking (e.g., Walmart, Honeywell).
* [**VeChain**](https://www.vechain.com/) - (Enterprise/Proprietary) A low-code blockchain-as-a-service platform used by BMW and DNV for tracking vehicle components and ensuring circularity. 3

## **Layer 1 Protocols (Green by Design)**

*Base-layer blockchains with low-carbon consensus mechanisms.*

* [**Algorand**](https://github.com/algorand) - A Pure Proof-of-Stake (PPoS) blockchain that is carbon-negative. The repository includes the core node software which is highly energy-efficient. 4
* [**Celo**](https://github.com/celo-org) - An EVM-compatible, carbon-negative blockchain focused on mobile-first financial inclusion and Regenerative Finance (ReFi).
* [**Chia Network**](https://github.com/Chia-Network/chia-blockchain) - Uses "Proof of Space and Time" to leverage unused hard drive space, reducing the energy waste associated with traditional mining.
* [**Circular Protocol**](https://circularlabs.io/) - A 4th-generation Layer 1 designed for compliance and sustainability in healthcare and waste management, utilizing "non-competitive mining" for energy efficiency. 5
* [**Hedera Services**](https://github.com/hashgraph/hedera-services) - The code powering the Hedera Hashgraph network, which offers high throughput with negligible energy consumption per transaction. 6

## **Data Oracles & Intelligence**

*Connecting real-world sensors to the blockchain.*

* [**Chainlink**](https://github.com/smartcontractkit/chainlink) - Critical for bringing weather data and satellite imagery onto blockchains to trigger "brilliant contracts" for crop insurance and carbon removal verification.
* [**Energy Web Credentials**](https://github.com/energywebfoundation/ew-credentials) - Libraries for managing Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) and Verifiable Credentials for energy assets.

*A spotlight on recent hackathon winners, pilot programs, academic proposals, and patents driving the next wave of green innovation.*

* [**Carbon Management Platform**](https://devpost.com/) *(Hedera "Hello Future" Winner 2025)* - An end-to-end verifiable carbon management platform that issues NFT-based carbon certificates and provides compliance-ready reporting for enterprises.

* [**ebitts (Enel x Conio)**](https://algorand.co/case-studies/enel-tokenizing-renewable-energy-assets-on-algorand) - A pilot by Enel (Europe's largest utility) on **Algorand**. * [**CarbonX 2.0 Finalist**](https://static.www.tencent.com/attachments/ssv/2025/CarbonX%202.0%20Finalists.pdf) *(CarbonX 2.0 Finalist)* - A pilot in Kenya selected by Tencent's CarbonX program, focusing on Direct Air Capture (DAC) powered by renewable geothermal energy, integrating digital verification for carbon removal credits.
